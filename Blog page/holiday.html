Wight Holiday Home

This handsome double fronted Victorian home was bought as a weekend escape for its new owners, but the layouts needed reconfiguring & updating in order to capitalise on the vews of the garden & the sea. An elegant extension was added to the back of the house and the kitchen & dining area were relocated here, and the dual aspect living room & games room make this the perfect home for entertaining family & friends. The owners wanted the house to be filled with jewel like colours, new en-suite bathrooms were installed throughout as well as a downstairs loo, a bootroom and a cinema room in the loft.
