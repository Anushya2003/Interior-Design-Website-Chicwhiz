Fulham Family Home.

This pretty terraced Fulham townhouse was bought by a family that were relocating from the US to the UK, and they wanted help in creating an elegant but comfortable home for their children and family to visit. We added a mix of timeless furniture & contemporary lighting to their existing art and created contrast in the newly decorated rooms by adding dark tones & finishes. A new study & loft suite created additional space, the bathrooms & kitchen were redesigned and new flooring, curtains and bespoke joinery was fitted throughout. The house is now a restful and elegant space that is filled with light and is the perfect place for relaxing with their family & entertaining guests.
