Georgian Townhouse.

This beautiful historic 4 storey townhouse was in need of some serious TLC when its new owners bought it. Rotten casement windows were replaced with historically correct wooden versions, floors were stripped and stained, ceiling roses were reinstated and wiring brought up to date. It is now an elegant but unpretentious house for a family of 5 that combines beautiful antiques with contemporary furniture, and a wonderful collection of art and family heirlooms..
