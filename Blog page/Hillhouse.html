Herne Hill Home.

This lovely Edwardian house was split into two flats when its owners bought it, and they wanted help restoring its grand proportions and period details whilst giving it a contemporary update. Bold architectural glazing and external panelling was complimented by bespoke joinery, a bespoke kitchen & modern lighting. Bursts of strong colour & pattern contrast with more traditional finishes, and reflect the owners passion for Art and interest in design.
